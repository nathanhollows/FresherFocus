Markdown and shortcuts

Typography

Headers

Headers in notes are automatically converted to a table of contents for both students and cases. Use ctrl+] to increase the header level (up to 6), ctrl+[ to decrease the header level (down to none) 

                
# Heading 1
## Heading 2
### Heading 3
#### Heading 4
##### Heading 5
###### Heading 6

Text

ctrl+b to make text bold ctrl+i to make text italic ctrl+u to underline text
